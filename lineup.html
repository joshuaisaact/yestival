Your YESTIVAL Lineup Guide


Friday


We'll be kicking off Friday with a MEGA B2B2B2B2B+, a big welcome to Yestival from all your weekend DJs, followed by two special hours of B2B MAGIC from some members of our crew.

Then A.OG and SAMI will be getting the party started with some soulful world housey vibes before our OPENING CEREMONY where NAWTY NICK and BONKER$BEN will light the fires of YESTIVAL and turn the heat up!

The rest of the evening will be all about the best in house and techno with the mighty DOLOMITE, TUDDS, MOTORIUS and SANCHEZ bringing it hard.

Closing the evening will be our secret SPECIAL GUEST TBA!



Saturday


Saturday afternoon is all about disco, world, funk and soul, a lovely 3 hour block in the sunshine brought to you by DJ ALLYLUJAH, BesTee and TOM THE DJ

Things get eclectic with the majest of MICHAEL CLAYTON LIVE and get rowdy with some BALKAN, EGG + CHIPS before we all come together to catwalk and find out the winners of our FANCY DRESS COMPETITION

Launching Yestopians into the evening will be the man himself, get hardcore with NAWTY NICK: LIFT OFF. Following on from his undeniable Bristol O2 Academy headline set it's CAPITAL T and we will keep pushing the tempo throughout the night

PJ HIPS: FOLKTRONIA RITAL will be bringing the sunset vibes, followed by the housey bassy garage fusions of SOUNDBOY COOKIE and INGA AURUM

And that's right, we NEED jungle and don't worry because your jungle tastebuds will be salivated by DADDY LONG LEGS, before the DnB essential bonk 2 bonk bangers of BONKER$BEN PRESENTS BONKTOPIA

Late night antics will be provided by dnb/dubstep king GASWELL and prepare for 130bpm fireworks with DJ DANK EVIL to close out action on the main stage!
