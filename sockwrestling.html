Sock Wrestling: KING OF THE RING

For those that are bold enough, enter the sacred circle for our knockout sock wrestling tourney to determine the Yestival King of the Ring! 💪🫅🏻

Lots will be drawn and a bracket created. First to unsheathe the opponents sock will advance to the next round.

The winner will receive a crown and must be addressed as Your Majesty for the rest of the weekend…. 👑
