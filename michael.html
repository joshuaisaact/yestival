There is a sacred game that represents everything great about the spirit of Yestival.

Wander down into the woods to find a customised glow in the dark / UV lit ping pong table, complete with glowing balls to play the most special of games…

In simple terms…It’s ping pong, outside, with 6 players, 2 have badminton rackets.

Winning isn’t important but Normalisation of play is essential. And you occasionally should shout shoop de woopa.

Hallucinogenics are optional but have been known to result in optimal performance.
